About Us

Peak Pressure Wash

At Peak Pressure Wash, we didn’t just stumble into the world of exterior cleaning—it’s something we’ve grown into with passion and dedication. After years of working in home repair, construction, and exterior projects, what started as a simple add-on service—cleaning decks, fences, and homes—has now become our main focus. Today, we’re all about making sure your property looks its best, no matter the season.

We know that maintaining the exterior of your home or business can be a challenge, which is why we offer custom cleaning schedules—whether you need us weekly, monthly, or quarterly, during the business day or after hours, we’ll be there to keep your property looking pristine all year round.

But what really drives us is building long-term relationships with our clients. For us, it’s not just about the one-time job—it’s about being the team you can count on for all your pressure washing needs, time and time again. We take pride in delivering the kind of service that makes a real difference, ensuring you can always rely on Peak Pressure Wash to keep your property in top shape.
