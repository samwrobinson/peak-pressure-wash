Residential Services

Residential Pressure Washing & Exterior Cleaning Services

Restore Your Home’s Beauty with Peak Pressure Wash

Your home is one of your most valuable investments, and maintaining its exterior is key to preserving curb appeal and preventing long-term damage. At Peak Pressure Wash, we offer a full range of residential pressure washing and exterior cleaning services designed to keep every surface of your home spotless, safe, and looking brand new.

Our Residential Services Include:

House Washing

Over time, dirt, mold, and mildew can accumulate on your home’s siding, dulling its appearance and potentially causing damage. Our professional house washing service safely removes grime and buildup, restoring your home’s beauty. Using a low-pressure soft washing technique, we ensure your home’s exterior is cleaned without damaging delicate surfaces like vinyl, wood, or stucco.

Roof Cleaning

Dark streaks, moss, and algae not only make your roof look unsightly but can also shorten its lifespan. Our asphalt shingle roof cleaning service uses a gentle, low-pressure wash to remove harmful contaminants and restore your roof to its original condition—without causing any damage.

Surface Cleaning

Driveways, patios, and walkways are constantly exposed to the elements, leading to dirt, oil stains, and grime buildup. With our powerful concrete cleaning services, we’ll restore these surfaces to their original shine. Whether it’s your driveway, pool deck, sidewalk, pavers, or decorative stone, we use professional-grade equipment to ensure a deep and even clean every time.

Window Cleaning

Crystal-clear windows can transform the look of your home. Our professional window cleaning service ensures streak-free, spotless windows that allow more natural light to flood your space. Whether you need exterior window cleaning or inside-and-out service, we guarantee a sparkling result every time.

Gutter Whitening & Cleaning

Clogged gutters can lead to serious issues like water damage, foundation problems, and mold growth. Our gutter cleaning service ensures your gutters function properly by removing leaves, debris, and blockages. We also offer gutter whitening to restore the clean, bright look of your gutter exteriors—improving curb appeal and preventing stains that can lead to long-term discoloration.

Fence & Deck Restoration

Over time, wood fences and decks can become weathered and dull. Our fence and deck cleaning service brings them back to life by removing dirt, mildew, and grime. We also offer staining and sealing services to protect your wood surfaces from UV damage and prolong their lifespan.

Specialty Service for Your Home

In addition to our core services, Peak Pressure Wash offers a variety of specialty services to address the unique needs of your property. Whether you’re dealing with tough rust stains, graffiti, or need a deep clean for your vinyl awnings, we have the solutions.

  • Rust Stain Treatment
  • Efflorescence Treatment
  • Vinyl Awning Cleaning
  • Graffiti Removal
  • Concrete Staining, Sealing, and Texturing
